My History with Frisbee

    Pictures Home About

I got into frisbee around my junior year of high school. I had a friend looking to create a club team, and was getting sick of tennis each spring. So instead, I joined the casual team and had a blast for the year, despite being severly undermanned. To combat this, we did some hardcore recruiting senior year and filled out a team complete with reserves. Due to Covid, my high school season got cut short. The college team at Saint Anselm gave me the first experience in a tourney, which was extremely fun while also frustrating because of losses.
