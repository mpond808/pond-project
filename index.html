    • Ultimate Frisbee is a sport played between two teams, with one frisbee between both of them. Unlike a lot of sports, it plays more like a backyard recreational game than an official sport, but it is fun nonetheless. It strikes the perfect balance between a competitive game and a casual and fun experience.
